About the Medicine Reminder App

The Medicine Reminder App is designed to help users manage their medication schedules efficiently. With an intuitive interface and robust features, our app ensures you never forget your pills again. It offers personalized notifications, a comprehensive medication tracker, and cloud backup to keep your health data safe. Whether you're managing a single prescription or multiple medications, our app makes it easier than ever.

We use Supabase for secure data storage, ensuring that all medication details are kept safe and accessible only to the user. The notification system is built using Flutter Local Notifications and Provider to ensure seamless alert delivery. The app's clean and responsive UI makes it user-friendly for people of all ages.
